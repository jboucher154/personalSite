Jenny Boucher

about Jenny.

A nurse, lover of all things crafty, and lifelong learner, Jenny hails from Orcas Island, Washington and currently resides in the Helsinki metropolitan area. Jenny completed her Bachelor's of Science in Nursing in 2010 and, following graduation, served in the US Army as a Nurse Corps Officer. In the army, she worked in various positions including surgical telemetry, hospital education, and labor and delivery. In 2018 Jenny left military service to pursue further education. In 2021 she graduated from the University of British Columbia with her Master's of Science in Nursing. Currently, Jenny is seeking to transfer her experience in the health care industry to the technology sector with a passion for serving patients and healthcare workers through the technology that shapes our current healthcare landscape.
